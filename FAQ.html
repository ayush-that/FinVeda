Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sevy?
Sevy is an educational platform designed to foster a ‘think like a trader’ mindset. It provides students with interactive tools to learn about financial markets, science, and space, using AI-powered simulations and games.
Do I need coding knowledge to use Sevy?
No, Sevy is designed for both coders and non-coders. Our platform allows you to explore trading and educational tools without the need for prior coding experience.
How do AI tools work in Sevy?
Our AI tools provide personalized learning experiences and simulate real-world trading environments. This helps users better understand market dynamics and develop a trader's mindset through practice.
What topics does Sevy cover?
Sevy covers a wide range of topics, including financial trading, space theories, environmental issues, and science. We aim to create educational games and simulations to make learning more engaging and interactive.
Is Sevy suitable for beginners?
Yes! Sevy is built for users of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. Whether you're learning about trading, space, or science, Sevy provides the tools to guide you step by step.
Can I use Sevy on my mobile device?
Yes, Sevy is fully responsive and can be used on any mobile device. You can access all features of the platform through your smartphone or tablet.
Is there a fee to use Sevy?
Sevy offers both free and premium content. You can explore a wide range of resources for free, and we also offer premium tools for those who want a more advanced learning experience.
What makes Sevy different from other educational platforms?
Sevy combines traditional learning with interactive AI tools and simulations to make education more engaging. We focus on cultivating a trader's mindset and provide unique content related to space, science, and environmental issues, making it a one-stop platform for diverse educational needs.
How can I contact the Sevy support team?
You can contact us by emailing support@sevy.com or by using the contact form on our website’s ‘Contact Us’ page. We are here to help you with any queries or issues you may have.
