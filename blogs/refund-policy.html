Refund Policy


Last updated:

We strive to ensure customer satisfaction and transparency in our refund process. Below is our detailed refund policy:


Eligibility for Refunds:

  • Subscription Plans: Refunds are available only for cancellations made within 7 days of purchase, provided no content or service has been accessed.
  • One-Time Payments: Refunds for one-time tool purchases are assessed on a case-by-case basis and are subject to verification.

Non-Refundable Items:

  • Digital Products: Downloadable tools, guides, or materials are non-refundable once accessed.
  • AI Consultation Services: Sessions or personalized reports are non-refundable once delivered.

Refund Process:

To request a refund, please contact our support team at ayush1337@hotmail.com. Provide your purchase details and the reason for your request. Once we receive your request, we will review it and notify you of the approval or rejection within 3-5 business days.


Refund Timeline:

If your refund is approved, the amount will be credited back to your original payment method within 10 business days. Please note that bank or credit card processing times may vary and might take additional time to reflect in your account.


Need Assistance?

For any queries or assistance regarding refunds, reach out to our support team at ayush1337@hotmail.com.
