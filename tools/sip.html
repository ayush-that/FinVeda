Tools

Mutual Fund Investment Calculator

Estimate your potential returns for SIP or LumpSum investments.

Here's how it works:
  1. Monthly Investment: Enter the amount you plan to invest monthly.
  2. Time Period (years): Specify the number of years for the investment.
  3. Expected Return Rate (p.a.): Enter the annual return rate you expect.
  4. Calculate: Computes the total investment, wealth gained, and maturity value based on inputs.

EMI Calculator

Calculate the monthly installment amount for a loan.

Here's how it works:
  1. Loan Amount: Enter the principal amount of the loan.
  2. Interest Rate: Specify the annual interest rate.
  3. Loan Tenure (months): Enter the loan period in months.
  4. Calculate EMI: Provides the monthly EMI, total payment, and total interest.

Savings Goal Calculator

Determine how much to save monthly to reach your goals.

Here's how it works:
  1. Goal Amount: Enter your financial goal amount.
  2. Current Savings: Enter the amount you have already saved.
  3. Monthly Savings: Specify the amount you plan to save monthly.
  4. Interest Rate: Enter the expected annual interest rate.
  5. Calculate Savings Goal: Calculates the time needed to reach your goal.

Insurance Calculator

Estimate insurance policy benefits based on inputs.

Here's how it works:
  1. Sum assured: Enter the sum assured amount.
  2. Policy term: Enter the policy term in years.
  3. Annual premium: Enter the annual premium percentage.
  4. Reversionary Bonus: Enter the reversionary bonus percentage.
  5. Terminal Bonus: Enter the terminal bonus percentage.
  6. Calculate Insurance: Calculates the insurance benefits based on the inputs.

Net Present Value Calculator

Evaluate the profitability of an investment by calculating the present value of expected future cash flows.

Here's how it works:
  1. Initial Investment: Enter the amount of your initial investment.
  2. Discount Rate: Enter the discount rate, which represents the rate of return required to make the investment worthwhile.
  3. Cash Flows: Add the expected cash flows for each year of the investment. You can add multiple cash flow inputs for different years and remove any as needed.
  4. Calculate NPV: Once all inputs are provided, click the 'Calculate NPV' button to compute the net present value. The result will display the NPV, indicating the potential profitability of your investment.

Return on Investment(ROI) Calculator

The ROI Calculator helps you measure the profitability of an investment.

Here's how it works:
  1. Initial Investment: Enter the initial investment amount.
  2. Final Value: Enter the final value of the investment.
  3. Calculate ROI: Determines the return on investment percentage.

Goods and Service Tax (GST) Calculator

This tool simplifies the calculation of GST for various transactions.

Here's how it works:
  1. Original Amount: Enter the original amount before GST.
  2. GST Rate: Specify the GST rate.
  3. Calculate GST: Computes the GST-inclusive or exclusive amount.

Profit Margin Calculator

This calculates the profit margin percentage based on the revenue and cost inputs.

Here's how it works:
  1. Cost Price: Enter the cost price of the product.
  2. Selling Price: Enter the selling price of the product.
  3. Calculate Profit Margin: Computes the profit margin based on the inputs.

Expenditure and Savings Calculator

This Calculator helps you analyze your monthly savings based on your income and expenses.

Here's how it works:
  1. Income: Enter your total monthly income.
  2. Expenses: List your monthly expenses.
  3. Calculate Savings: Analyzes and provides your monthly savings.

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) Calculator

The total monetary value of all the final goods and services produced within the geographic boundaries of a country, during a given period (usually a year).

Here's how it works:
  1. Personal Consumption: Enter the total household spending on goods and services.
  2. Gross Investment: Input the total business and household investment in capital assets.
  3. Government Consumption: Provide the total government expenditure on goods and services.
  4. Exports: Enter the total value of goods and services sold abroad.
  5. Imports: Input the total value of goods and services purchased from other countries.
  6. Calculate: Click the "Calculate" button to compute the GDP by summing the inputs and adjusting for net exports.
  7. Clear: Click the "Clear" button to reset the form and enter new data.

Systematic Withdrawal Plan Calculator

This helps investors plan and to manage the regular withdrawal of funds from their investment corpus, typically mutual funds.

Here's how it works:
  1. Total Investment: Enter your total investment.
  2. Withdrawal Per Month: Enter your withdrawal per month.
  3. Expected Return Rate (p.a): Enter your expected return rate (p.a).
  4. Total Period (in years): Enter your total period of investment in years.
  5. Remaining Amount: Analyzes and provides the remaining balance of your investment after scheduled withdrawals, total amount withdrawn, and the duration the investment will last.

Credit Card Payoff Calculator

Calculate how long it will take to pay off your credit card balance with this calculator.

Here's how it works:
  1. Credit Card Balance: Enter your current credit card balance.
  2. Annual Percentage Rate (APR): Input the annual interest rate on your credit card.
  3. Monthly Payment: Enter the amount you can pay each month.

Split Bill Calculator

Calculate how much each person should pay when splitting a bill, including tips.

Here's how it works:
  1. Bill Subtotal: Enter the total amount of the bill before the tip.
  2. Tip Percentage: Specify the percentage of the bill you would like to add as a tip.
  3. Number of Persons: Enter the number of people who will be splitting the bill.

Fixed Deposit Calculator

It compute the maturity amount and interest earned on fixed deposits based on the principal, interest rate, and duration.

Here's how it works:
  1. Principal Amount (₹): Enter your principal amount.
  2. Annual Interest Rate (%): Enter your annual interest rate.
  3. Total Period (in years): Enter your total period of investment in years.
  4. Total Interest Earned: Analyzes and provides the total interest earned on your investment.
  5. Total Maturity Amount: Analyzes and provides total maturity amount.

Recurring Deposit Calculator

It computes the maturity amount and interest earned on regular monthly deposits over a fixed period, given a specified annual interest rate.

Here's how it works:
  1. Monthly Installment (₹): Enter your monthly installment amount.
  2. Annual Interest Rate (%): Enter your annual interest rate.
  3. Total Period (in months): Enter your total period of investment in months.
  4. Total Interest Earned: Analyzes and provides the total interest earned on your investment.
  5. Total Maturity Amount: Analyzes and provides total maturity amount.

Employee Provident Fund Calculator

It helps employees estimate their total savings at retirement by factoring in monthly salary, age, contribution rate, annual salary increase, and interest rate.

Here's how it works:
  1. Monthly Salary (Basic + DA): Enter your monthly salary (Basic + DA).
  2. Your Age: Enter your age.
  3. Your Contribution to EPF (%): Enter your contribution to EPF.
  4. Annual Increase in Salary (%): Enter your annual increase in salary.
  5. Rate of Interest (%): Enter your rate of interest.
  6. Total EPF at retirement: Analyzes and provides the total EPF at retirement.
